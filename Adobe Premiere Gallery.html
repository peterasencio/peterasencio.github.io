Freedom

My group will produce a short film about all the freedoms that are being taken away from us . One of the characters will represent the youth of this country and show that  we need to act and make a difference to become free. Another character will represent the outcome of having their freedoms taken away. During the film the character will have a slow progression of their freedom taken away. The character that  represents the youth will go through different obstacles in order to become free.
